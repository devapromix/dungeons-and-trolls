After two months of travel, you have arrived in Elvion. A forgotten land beyond the mountains, where the Troll King has risen once again from the darkness. His armies march — silent, dark, and inevitable.

There is only one hope — the Sword of Dawn. A legendary weapon wielded over a hundred years ago by a hero who tried to stop the evil. He vanished in the Troll Cave, deep within the heart of the Troll Temple. And the sword disappeared with him.
